CoastalIQ — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026
App: CoastalIQ (com.coastaliq.app)
Provider: Teknotics, LLC · info@teknotics.com

What this app does

CoastalIQ provides inshore tide, weather, and fishing-condition forecasts, lets you save locations, log trips (with optional photos), and receive tide notifications.

Information we process

Location (precise). When you use “current location” or select a forecast point, your coordinates are sent over HTTPS to forecast data sources (NOAA Tides & Currents, NWS weather) and our forecasting backend to return predictions. Coordinates are used only to fulfill that request and are not stored on our servers. Saved locations are kept on your device only.

Photos you attach to trips. If you add a catch photo, the image may be uploaded over HTTPS to our backend for automated catch recognition (species/measurement assistance). The image is processed and then discarded — we do not store it on our servers. Photos you keep in a trip log are stored on your device only.

Trip / plan content. Trip-planning inputs you submit for “plan assist” are sent over HTTPS to our backend to generate suggestions, then discarded — not stored on our servers. Your trip log itself stays on your device.

Notifications. Tide-alert scheduling happens on your device; no notification content is sent to us.

Accounts. The app currently has no sign-in / user accounts, so we do not collect names, emails, or login credentials. (A future release will add sign-in; this policy will be updated before that ships.)

We do NOT use third-party advertising, and the app contains no analytics or crash-reporting SDKs (no Firebase/Crashlytics/Sentry/etc.). We do not sell your data.

Third-party services

Requests may go to: NOAA Tides & Currents (tidesandcurrents.noaa.gov), NWS (api.weather.gov), OpenStreetMap and Esri (map tiles), and our AWS-hosted backend (CloudFront/Bedrock). Each receives only what’s needed to fulfill the request (e.g., coordinates, or an image for recognition) and our backend does not retain it.

Data security & retention

Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS). We do not store your uploaded photos, plan inputs, or location on our servers — they are processed transiently and discarded. On-device data (saved locations, trip logs, photos, preferences) stays in the app’s private storage until you delete it or uninstall the app.

Your choices & deletion

  • Revoke location/camera/photo/notification permissions anytime in OS settings.
  • Delete trips/photos/saved locations in-app; uninstalling removes all on-device data.
  • Because we do not store your content on our servers, there is no server-side copy to delete. Questions: info@teknotics.com.

Children

Not directed to children under 13; we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy; the effective date will change. Questions: info@teknotics.com.
